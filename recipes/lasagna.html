Lasagna Recipe

Home

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is a family favorite. This recipe has been handed down from generations in our family. We hope you enjoy it.

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese, 2 cups
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • ¾ cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • tsp pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • ¾ lb. ground beef
  • ¾ lb. ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 40 oz. marinara sauce, see notes
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon hot sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

Lasagna Noodles & Cheese Topping

  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked according to package instructions
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup Parmesan cheese, freshly grated
