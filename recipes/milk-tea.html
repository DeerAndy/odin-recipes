Milk Tea Recipe


Home

Milk Tea is a classic Taiwanese drink that is a favorite around the world. This recipe has been handed down from generations in our family. We hope you enjoy it.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black tea
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup tapioca pearls

Instructions

  1. Boil the tapioca pearls according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Prepare the black tea according to the package instructions. Add the brown sugar and stir until dissolved.
  3. Fill a glass with ice. Add the tapioca pearls, then pour the black tea over the top.
  4. Top with milk and stir to combine. Enjoy!
